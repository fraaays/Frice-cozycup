About Us

Frice-CozyCup Café is a warm and welcoming space created for coffee lovers who seek comfort, quality, and connection in every cup. Inspired by cozy moments and carefully crafted brews, our café offers a relaxing atmosphere where friends can gather, students can focus, and individuals can unwind while enjoying delicious coffee, refreshing drinks, and delightful treats made with passion and care.
